How This Works

1. Choose Your Spot

Pick your session time and make a reservation online or via phone!

We highly recommend reservations as we have limited space and want to avoid disappointment.

2. Pick Your Project

Take a look at our current projects! We will have all the material as well as detailed instructions for each project ready for you.

3. Order a Drink & Get Crafting!

Get a drink from our bar and take your spot at your craft table. Our Craft Attendents will be around for any questions, guidance or to top up your drink!

4. Share the Fun

Share & Tag @craftbrewYYC on Facebook, Twitter & Instagram!

While most projects generally take the whole three hours, you are welcome to arrive within the first hour of your craft session.

The sessions are self-guided with a clear page of instructions available to you. If you need any assistance, our Craft Attendents are eager to help!

Walk-ins are welcome, but space is limited! Don't be disappointed and ensure you and your friends get a spot by reserving now!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How Does It Work? What Can I Expect?
    • Make a reservation.
    • Arrive within the first hour of your rerserved time.
    • Check-in with our front desk.
    • Pick a project from our project menu and open a tab with your Craft Attendent (if you like!).
    • Our Craft Attendent will help you collect your supplies and get you set up at your crafting station.
    • Order a drink, get comfortable and start creating!

    • ···

  • Do I Have to Make a Reservation?
    • We highly highly recommend reservations to avoid disappointment but we do sometimes have space available for walk-ins. Give us a call and we will be happy to tell you if there is space available!

    • ···

  • Do I Need Any Experience?
    • Our projects range in experience levels and our Craft Attendents are here to get you out of any sticky jams.

    • ···

  • What if I Hurt Myself?
    • We have first-aid materials on site and all of our Craft Attendents have been trained in the most recent First Aid techniques.

    • ···

  • Can I Bring My Own Booze?
    • Nope! Check out our drink menu for some amazing options!

    • ···

  • What About Food?
    • Absolutely! We would recommend keeping it small so you don't take up all your crafting space, but feel free to bring in food.
