1. Choose Your Spot

Pick your session time and make a reservation online or via phone!

We highly recommend reservations as we have limited space and want to avoid disappointment.

2. Pick Your Project

Take a look at our current projects! We will have all the material as well as detailed instructions for each project ready for you.

3. Order a Drink & Get Crafting!

Get a drink from our bar and take your spot at your craft table. Our Craft Attendent will be around for any questions, guidance or to top up your drink!

4. Share the Fun

Share & Tag @craftbrewYYC on Facebook, Twitter & Instagram!
