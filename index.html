Craft, Drink, Laugh & Repeat

Come join us for one of our craft sessions! We have the space, the tools and the supplies for all the projects on our project menu as well as some excellent cocktails, delicious wine and a selection of local craft beers.

While walk-ins are welcome, we do have limited spaces for each session! We highly recommend our crafters book in advance to secure their space!

Have more questions? Check out how this works, our FAQ or contact us.
