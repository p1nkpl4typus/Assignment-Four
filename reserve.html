Let's Craft Together!

The Fine Print:

  • Projects can take up to the whole three hours. We ask that you show up within the first hour of your crafting session to ensure you have enough time to complete your masterpiece.
  • A reservation is not a pre-paid ticket. We verify your credit card prior to your session starting and settle the bill at the end. Costs for drinks and projects vary. Gratuity is not included.
  • You can modify your reservation anytime up to 24 hours prior to your session time.
  • There is a $20 no show fee per person. If you reserve a party of six and only four show up, $40 will be added to your bill.
  • There is a 1 project per reserved seat purchase requirement.

Questions? Email us at reservations@craftbrewyyc.com or call us at 403 555 1234.
